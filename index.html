Bitcoin, often described as a cryptocurrency, a virtual currency or a digital currency - is a type of money that is completely virtual. It's like an online version of cash.

Ethereum is a blockchain-based platform that is best known for its cryptocurrency, ETH. The blockchain technology that powers Ethereum enables secure digital ledgers to be publicly created and maintained.

Litecoin (LTC) is a cryptocurrency that was founded in 2011, two years after Bitcoin, by a former Google engineer named Charlie Lee. Like Bitcoin, Litecoin is based on an open-source global payment network that is not controlled by any central authority.

Ripple is a technology that acts as both a cryptocurrency and a digital payment network for financial transactions. The token used for the cryptocurrency is premined and utilizes the ticker symbol XRP.
